Work

Birmingham City University

  • BackboneJS
  • MarionetteJS
  • jQuery
  • GruntJS
  • RequireJS
  • SASS

The Birmingham City University web platform project took place over 3 years and included a initial 6 month consultation and planning period, then 18 months of actual development and testing, all to bring together hundreds of satellite websites under one consistently branded web platform and CMS. Version 2.0 of the website was a visual refresh of the website developed over the previous 2 years.

Fully responsive templates were built using HTML, CSS and Javascript.

During this project, I acted as visual designer, user experience designer, client consultant, CMS trainer and front-end developer.

Youthspace

  • BackboneJS
  • MarionetteJS
  • jQuery
  • GruntJS
  • RequireJS
  • SASS

Youthspace is a service within Birmingham & Solihull Mental Health Trust that acts as an information and help point for young people with mental health issues. Youthspace required an online presence that was suitable for their young target audience but professional enough to represent the subject matter. Information architecture planning was paramount in a website where the user finding the right information quickly and easily was the main priority.

During this project, I acted as visual designer, information architect, and both front-end and back-end developer.
